FAQs

What is Intelligere?
Intelligere is an accounting/bookkeeping automation Software. It is not a book-keeping/accounting software nor ERP software.
Will my data be used by Intelligere or by their team?
Only upon your consent your data will be used by Digital Documentation Systems team for advanced analytics for your own benefits.
What kind of files can be imported into Intelligere?

Only Electronic PDF (not scanned or image-based PDF) and CSV files can be used and imported in Intelligere.

On what accounting/Bookkeeping principles Intelligere works?

Intelligere works on Double entry accounting/bookkeeping principles.

How does Intelligere work?

Intelligere analyses the imported documents, extracts relevant data, through our intelligent processes and shows the data for minimum changes from the user’s side.

Once the user makes changes in data and submits it, the data goes into the user’s official bookkeeping/accounting software via integlligere.

What is the accuracy level of data extraction with Intelligere?

Intelligere aims to deliver highest possible accuracy so that manual data entry work can be avoided as much as possible only checking of the data is required.

As of now we claim to have achieved almost 85%+ accuracy with our intelligent backend processes if the imported data is very well formatted. But then it also depends on type and kind of data which is being fed to Intelligere.

What modules are offered in Intelligere as of now?

Bank Statement, Invoices (Sales and Purchase) as well as Cheque modules are right now available. Intelligere is a work in progress to make many more accounts related documents accessible in Intelligere.

What kind of data security is in place in Intelligere?

We have provided Bank level security in Intelligere, so be rest assured about your data. It is very safe with us.

What if I want to discontinue using Intelligere? What would happen to my financial data

Any time you can discontinue using Intelligere. Be rest assured Your data would remain completely safe as we push your data automatically in your accounting software as well, every time you import any of the documents, if you have chosen to connect it with Intelligere at the time of signup.

Your data with us will also remain totally secure till you choose to end our relationship and give us consent to delete it from our end or 30 days has passed and if you have discontinued our relationships and haven’t given consent to delete the data, it will be assumed that you no longer wish that we should keep your data. Once the data is deleted Intelligere won’t have any access to any deleted data, even if you wish to retrieve the data along with pdf files. Hence, please be very clear when you decide to delete it. You will be given choice to download all your pdf files on to your local PC.

Will my data be shared or visible to any other users?

We highly value the secrecy of your financial data. Hence, with our complete and total secure systems, one user would not be able to see or access any other users’ data at all.

What if I make changes directly in my accounting software?

Even if you directly make some changes in your accounting software, your changes will remain in there. Changes made in your accounting software remains final.

What if my accounting software is not in your list of accounting software with which Intelligere can be connected?

We are trying to get connected to as many generally popular accountings software as possible, hence you can always connect with us/send us feedback to us with the name of your software and we will try our best to make it happen at our earliest.

Why do I need to give my bank details and GST, PAN etc details in Intelligere?

For your convenience, once you fill up your data, every time your details are required, Intelligere automatically fills in those details.

How do I operate Intelligere?

Intelligere is a very simple, user-friendly & intuitive software, yet, in case of any difficulties, we have provided collapsing help bar on right side of each page of the software, for that particular page functions, which is a very unique and helpful concept to ease users facing issues. Plus, we have added short videos of each page functioning in our website to help user navigate swiftly through Intelligere.

How do I start to use Intelligere?

You must register and sign-up, on the same pc where registered copy of Tally is installed.

What do I do once I register my company on Inteligere?

Welcome aboard! once your company is registered in Intelligere, please go to admin panel, at the left bottom corner and click on it, there create users and fill in all other details. Finally go to select bookkeeping software and select Tally ERP-9. Once selected, an exe file will be downloaded on to the same PC with Installed Tally. Click on it and follow the instructions to install it completely.

What to do if .EXE file is not downloaded or installed?

If on your computer you have an anti-virus or firewall installed, then temporarily you will have to disable it to install .EXE file.

What to do once the .EXE file is installed?

Congratulations! now you are ready to start using Intelligere.

Would Intelligere work on any Tally?

We recommend you to use only licenced copy of Tally for best results.
